How to Make Any Mug Scramble

  • Step 1: Spray mug with cooking spray or add a tiny bit of oil
  • Step 2: Add harder vegetables (broccoli, peppers) and microwave 30-60 seconds
  • Step 3: Pour in egg whites (and whole egg if using)
  • Step 4: Add softer ingredients (spinach, cheese, pre-cooked meats)
  • Step 5: Microwave 90 seconds, stir, then 30-60 seconds more until set
  • Step 6: Add final toppings (avocado, salsa, hot sauce) and enjoy!

Macro Tips for Mugs

  • Egg whites: ~25 g protein / 120 cal per cup
