Microwave Mug Scramble Cheat Sheet

Use this guide to build high-protein, low-calorie mug scrambles that fit a fat-loss & muscle-gain plan.

Each combo assumes ~1 cup liquid egg whites (~120 cal, 25g protein). Add 1 whole egg for richness (+70 cal, +6g protein).
